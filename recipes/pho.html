Pho Recipe

Looking for a something warm on a chilly day or perhaps something to cure your hangover? I have the perfect solution which is a very popular vietnamese soup dish called Pho. Consisting of rice noodles, beef, herbs, and spices all in a rich beef bone broth.It'll satisfy your body and your mind.

Below me is a detailed list and step by step guide on how to make this delicious fixing.

    ~Ingredients~

  • Pho Rice Noodles
  • 3lbs Brisket
  • 2lbs Meaty Bones(I prefer oxtail)
  • Ginger
  • Rock Sugar
  • Salt
  • Fish Sauce
  • Onion
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Coriander
  • Star Anise
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Thai Basil
  • Sriacha
  • Hoison Sauce
  • Lime

    ~Steps~

  1. Boil your meat and bones for 5 minutes
  2. Extract scum as it surfaces
  3. Drain and rinse your meat and bones of all the scum
  4. Char Onion and Ginger
  5. Toast your spices
  6. Fill pot with bones,meats, toasted spices, and the charred ginger and onions and simmer for 3 hours
  7. Remove brisket and thinly slice
  8. Simmer for another 40 minutes with just the bones
  9. Strain the broth of the spices and onion and ginger
  10. Season with fish sauce, sugar, and salt until disired taste
  11. Boil noodles
  12. Once cooked to desired doneness, place in a bowl.
  13. Pour broth over noodles with herbs, meats, and sauces
  14. Enjoy!
